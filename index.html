I am a Machine Learning researcher currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU). My work focuses on deep learning for medical imaging and applied machine learning for healthcare and text analysis.

I develop end-to-end ML pipelines including data preprocessing, model training, transfer learning, performance evaluation, and explainability techniques such as Grad-CAM. I also work on NLP tasks such as spam detection, text classification, and sentiment analysis.

I have published research in peer-reviewed journals and built reproducible machine learning systems for cancer image classification, pneumonia detection, and mental health sentiment analysis.
