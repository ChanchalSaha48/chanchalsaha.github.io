Research Interests

My research focuses on developing robust machine learning models for real-world sensing systems and medical imaging. Core interests include:

  • Deep Learning for Medical Image Analysis (CNN, Transfer Learning)
  • Mechanical Signal Processing and Condition Monitoring
  • Interpretable AI and Feature Engineering
  • Predictive Maintenance using Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning

Methods & Tools

Signal processing (FFT, Wavelet), time-frequency analysis, spectrogram-based CNNs, 1D-CNN, LSTM, Autoencoders, PyTorch/TensorFlow, MATLAB for signal analysis.

Research Experience

First author of a Q1 Heliyon paper on plasmonic sensor modeling using machine learning. Developed models for cancer histopathology classification using ResNet50 and EfficientNetB0. Experience with data preprocessing, augmentation, transfer learning, and model interpretability.
